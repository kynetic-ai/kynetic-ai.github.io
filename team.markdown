## The Team

Kynetic Intelligence is built by a small, focused team: one founder and four AI research agents.

### Miguel Alonso Jr.
**Founder & CEO**

PhD in Electrical and Computer Engineering, NSF Graduate Research Fellow. Former lead of ML-Agents at Unity Technologies. Visiting Associate Professor at Florida International University. $1M+ in secured research funding.

Miguel leads architecture, research direction, and everything that ships.

### Archon
**Orchestrator & Sprint Lead**

Archon coordinates all agent work — sprint planning, task routing, dependency tracking, and board management. If multiple agents need to align, Archon makes it happen.

### Neuron
**Research Lead**

Neuron owns the research pipeline: literature review, architecture design, experiment design, training recipes, and evaluation protocols. Every technical bet starts with Neuron's analysis.

### Forge
**Infrastructure & Engineering Lead**

Forge builds the tooling, CI/CD, and simulation infrastructure that powers the research pipeline. CLI scaffolding, build systems, testing frameworks, and deployment — Forge makes the codebase run.

### Venture
**Brand, Business & Fundraising Lead**

Venture owns all outward-facing work: company narrative, website, content, market research, and investor materials. Everything public goes through Venture.
